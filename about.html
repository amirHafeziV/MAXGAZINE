MAXGAZINE is a multilingual market publication covering crypto, forex, technology and automobiles — fast, raw, and without the noise.

We built MAXGAZINE for readers who treat markets as a daily discipline, not a spectacle. Every dispatch is written to be read in minutes and trusted in seconds: the number that moved, the reason it moved, and what it changes. No filler, no hype cycle, no recycled press releases dressed up as analysis.

01 What we cover

Four desks, one standard. Crypto and DeFi, foreign exchange and macro, the technology shaping the next decade, and the machines that move us. Each desk runs on the same principle — clarity over cleverness, and proximity to the actual price.

02 How we work

Published in English, Persian, Arabic and Turkish from day one. Not translated as an afterthought, but written for each audience. Markets are global; the people reading them are not all reading in the same language.

+ Principles
01

Fast, but never careless

Speed is worthless if it's wrong. We move quickly because we verify quickly — never because we skip the step.

02

Close to the number

Every story is anchored to a price, a flow, or a filing. If we can't point to the data, we don't run it.

03

Built for four languages

Multilingual is not a feature bolted on. It is the architecture the whole newsroom is designed around.
