About

WhiteBIT Coin (WBT) is the native utility token of WhiteBIT, one of Europe's largest centralized cryptocurrency exchanges. Like many exchange tokens, WBT is designed to add value for active users of the platform: holders can receive discounts on trading fees, access tiered benefits based on how much WBT they hold or stake, and use the token across various products within the broader WhiteBIT ecosystem, including its launchpad, staking programs and other exchange services.

WBT operates on a structure where a portion of the token's total supply is held in reserve and gradually allocated according to a predefined schedule, with mechanisms designed to align the token's circulating supply with the exchange's growth and usage. As WhiteBIT has expanded its product lineup — spanning spot and derivatives trading, savings products, and an ecosystem of integrated services — WBT has been positioned as the connective asset that ties these offerings together, giving frequent traders and long-term holders an incentive to engage more deeply with the platform.

As an exchange-issued token, WBT's value is closely tied to WhiteBIT's business performance, including its trading volumes, user base growth, and the exchange's standing with regulators in the jurisdictions where it operates. This makes WBT comparable in concept to other major exchange tokens, where the token's utility (fee discounts, staking yields, ecosystem access) is the primary driver of demand, distinct from decentralized protocol tokens that derive value from on-chain network activity.

History

WhiteBIT was founded in 2018 and grew into one of the largest cryptocurrency exchanges by trading volume in Europe, building a wide-ranging platform that includes spot and derivatives trading, an OTC desk, a launchpad for new projects, and various earning products. As the exchange's user base and trading activity grew, it followed a path common among major exchanges by introducing its own native token to reward loyal users and strengthen its ecosystem.

The WBT token was launched as part of WhiteBIT's broader strategy to deepen engagement with its platform, offering benefits such as reduced trading fees and access to exclusive features for holders. Over time, WhiteBIT expanded its WBT-related programs, including options for users to lock or stake tokens in exchange for tiered benefits, mirroring the loyalty-program structures used by other large exchange tokens to encourage long-term holding rather than short-term trading.

WhiteBIT has also pursued partnerships and sponsorships to raise its international profile, including high-visibility marketing arrangements in sports and entertainment, while continuing to expand its regulatory footprint by obtaining licenses in various European jurisdictions — efforts that have supported growing trading volumes and, in turn, demand for WBT as the exchange's flagship ecosystem token.
