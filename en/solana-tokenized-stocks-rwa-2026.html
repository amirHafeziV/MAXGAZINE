# Solana Captures 95% of Tokenized Stock Market as RWA Ecosystem Hits $3B Record

Solana processed $1.298 billion of the $1.324 billion in global tokenized equity trading volume during the week of June 15–21, 2026 — a 95% market share that no single blockchain has achieved before in any asset class at this scale. Simultaneously, Solana's real-world asset (RWA) ecosystem crossed the $3.1 billion total value locked (TVL) mark, capping 18 months of 1,500% growth. These milestones arrived in the same week that Bitcoin fell to its lowest levels since September 2024, underscoring a quiet but significant shift in where institutional and retail attention is moving.

Solana's RWA Ecosystem Surpasses $3 Billion

Solana's RWA TVL stood below $200 million at the start of 2025. By late June 2026, it had reached $3.1 billion. On June 10, daily transfer volume hit a record $1.49 billion — evidence that this is active capital, not idle deposits.

xStocks, developed by Backed Finance and launched on Solana in June 2025, accounts for roughly 93% of tokenized equity volume on the network. More than 200 stocks from global companies are now available as on-chain tokens. For the first time, a retail investor in any country can trade Apple or Microsoft shares 24/7 without a U.S. brokerage account — a structural change with implications far beyond crypto.

The SpaceX Token That Made $50 Million in 24 Hours

The tokenization of securities is not new. What is new is the velocity. The SpaceX tokenized stock (SPCX), launched on the Backpack platform in early 2026, recorded over $50 million in trading volume within its first 24 hours — making it the highest-volume tokenized equity ever launched on Solana.

Backed Finance underpins the model with 100% physical asset backing: every xAAPL token corresponds to one real Apple share held in custody. This reduces counterparty risk but does not eliminate it. Retail investors previously locked out of pre-IPO and private-company shares can now participate via non-custodial wallets. The broader tokenized securities market surpassed $43 billion in June 2026 — still a fraction of global equity markets, but on a trajectory that makes the direction clear.

Why Solana Owns 95% of This Market

Ethereum still holds 58% of the total RWA market across all asset categories. But in tokenized equities specifically, Solana's lead is overwhelming. The reason is transaction speed and cost.

Solana processes up to 65,000 transactions per second at fees below $0.01. For assets that may trade dozens of times per day, this cost differential is decisive. Even Ethereum's Layer 2 networks charge fees that can reach several dollars under peak load. Beyond raw speed, Solana's DeFi ecosystem — Kamino, Jupiter, Orca — enables composability that traditional markets cannot offer: a trader can post xAAPL as collateral to borrow stablecoins in the same transaction. That combination of access, speed, and composability has no equivalent in traditional finance.

Conclusion

Solana has transformed tokenized stocks from a niche experiment into a $3 billion market. The risks are real: custody risk (will Backed Finance always hold the underlying asset?), regulatory risk (will the SEC classify these tokens as securities?), and liquidity risk in a sharp market downturn. These are not hypothetical concerns. Investors entering this space need to understand them clearly.

But the macro trajectory is set. Global equity markets will eventually tokenize, and the blockchain that captures the largest early share will define its ecosystem's relevance for the next decade. Solana's 95% market share in 2026 is the product of years of deliberate investment in speed and low cost. The question is no longer whether this market grows — it is who else joins Solana in it.
